#4 Exquisite Corpse

*move your mouse around*

Description

My exquisite corpse is a blue monster with a thousand eyes sitting on a chair. My artstyle is heavily influenced by Henri Émile Benoît Matisse and his paper-cutting pieces. The eyes of the monster would follow your cursor, always looking at you.

Design Process

This work is in a collaborate with sichun zhang, Yii.



We exchanged our drawing body parts. I did the head part, and got the upper and lower body parts from sichun and Yii. I only wanna take some elements in the painting, like the hands, the sitting and also I left out the tank top part because I wanted the whole work to look cohesive.

Reflection

I wanted to draw a monster with a thousand eyes, so I utilized the 'function' in p5.js combined with a loop function, thus I don't have to write the eye function over and over again.

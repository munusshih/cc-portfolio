Description

In this game, you will be playing young Munus in his 18. In the beginning, you have just become a college student majoring in Engineering, but you always have this passion for design. In the game, You have to choose between going to classes, preparing for exams or doing the design you love. If you choose design over classes, your GPA is gonna drop and eventually be expelled. If you choose classes over design, you will be depressed. So, Balancing is King.

The concept behind the game is to represent the difficulty of handling multiple aspects of one's life at the same time, and how doing the things you love is not enough, but balancing over multiple duty tasks, passion projects are the only way to survive.

Design Process

The original concept was to make a narrative game with multiple options to choose from, but the format or experience of playing such game doesn't really capture that difficulty and urgency of choosing and balancing in one's life.


In the end I took inspiration from games like Space Invader that would force the player to response immediately to the options and could see the gpa/depression number increases or decreases.
Having so friends tested the game for me, I found out that the interface right now is not very tuitive. I might have to find a more graphical way to visualize the gpa/depression number and make it easier for the users to make further actions.



Reflection

Making a game is so hard! It is a holisitc kind of user experience. There are still so many things to do after making a greybox, and the art of the game is KING.
I ran out of time but I might add more levels to the game or add music to it in the future. I learned a lot about games and definitely reflect hard on the games I have played during my process. It was a thought-provoking experience.

Description

The idea is to create a kaleidoscope that would change the way we percieve ourselves.

Design Process

I personally love kaleidoscope so much that it's one of my favorite toys growing up. Below are several screenshots I took when playing with it.


I love how it is changing the way we perceive reality. I remembered when I was little, I was amazed by how things seemed so different from the lens and how it transform ordinary life objects into mesmerizing combinations of shapes and colors.
Thus, I tried to recreate a kaleidoscope that is both interactive yet also changes with frameCount. It is constantly rotating and scaling, creating infinite amounts of variations.

Reflection

I am very happy with the result. This is exactly what I wanted and even more fun.
I eventually play with it for many hours (haha), and I even fixed the bug so it would work across different devices (even work on mobile!). I really hope the kaleidoscope would make some people's day and remind them what it's like to be a kid and always get excited to learn new things, see things with fresh eyes.

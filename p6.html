Description

I have been so busy for the past week, so I've decided to collect data about my weekly schedule. What I was doing in a certain time slot and how long have I been doing it.

Design Process

I started out searching for different data visualization types and ended up discovering this website called data visualization project that has a great variety of different data types. I also wanted to combine two different data viz methods: a timeline that represents when and what I was doing; stacked bar chart that totals the number of hours that I have been working.


I also drew inspiration from Candlestick and other data types. It feels rewarding to combine different types of data viz and create your own.

Reflection

It is interesting to see how by utilizing interactivity, the audience can have a better understanding of the data through the act of playing. It is also fascinating to think of how data can literally be anything as long as it could be quantitated or qualitated. Through different methods of mapping, designers can really tell any story with data visualization.
